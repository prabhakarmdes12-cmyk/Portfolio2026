Overview

In 201, LXRGuide was supporting small teams across the US in their marketing efforts. The tool helps users increase sales from their website, identifying gaps in marketing efforts and suggesting actionable recommendations. I led design for the LXRGuide Product Team at Netelixir; we launched the updated interface in August 2018.

Role

UX Designer — User Research, Interaction Design, Visual Design, Prototyping & Testing.

Process

Based on Double Diamond Theory and Lean UX: Discovery, Definition, Ideation, Implementation. I conducted research interviews with primary users (account managers) to uncover pain points with the beta release.

Key Outcomes

  • Reduced clicks and presented data on a single dashboard page
  • New optimizations viewing options by task type or campaign
  • Ads Grader, SEO Grader, Activity report, Performance report
  • Mobile app introduced for applying recommendations on the go

Result

LXRGuide was able to crunch 40 hours of work into 5 minutes a week. In the first 30 days, one client's overall website revenues went up 17.82% and paid search revenues increased 77%.

Learning

A dashboard should be clear, meaningful, consistent, and simple—presenting complex information in an easy-to-understand format. Users should grasp its purpose within the first 5 seconds.

Overview

LXRSEO: Your Website's SEO Scanner—measure and improve SEO performance with 62+ parameters.

Scope

Product Strategy, Information Architecture, User Experience Design.

My Role

As UX Designer I was responsible for the entire project: User persona development, card sorting workshop with stakeholders, usability testing with real users, implementing findings in final design, and visual design.

Research

Conducted interviews with CEO, co-founder, digital marketing experts, and real users. Domain research with WordStream Keyword Tool. Competitive analysis of 4 competitors in the SEO space. User research revealed challenges in showcasing SEO's value and measuring ROI.

Key Insights

  • Users desire automation for repetitive tasks
  • Steep learning curve—need for simplicity and clear data explanations
  • User-friendly onboarding crucial

Results

  • Increased free trial to paid conversion by 8%
  • User retention (13 weeks average) increased by 30%
  • Improved usability with actionable insights

Visual Design Principles

Clarity, Relevance, Coherence, Simplicity.

Project Type

SaaS Platform — Infrastructure Monitoring & Business Intelligence.

Overview

NetQ Command is a next-generation technical monitoring dashboard designed to bridge the gap between complex IT operations and business strategy. It transforms raw technical data into actionable business outcomes, allowing stakeholders to visualize infrastructure health through the lens of ROI and operational stability.

UX Innovations

  • Business Asset Topography: An interactive mapping system that translates technical nodes into logical business units.
  • Smart Translation Engine: Automatically maps technical metrics (like latency and uptime) to business impacts (like revenue stability and experience risk).
  • Simulation Mode: A storytelling feature for incident response training and platform value demonstration.
  • ROI Visibility: Real-time tracking of "Loss Prevented" and "Savings Opportunities" based on system performance.

Design Approach

The interface uses the Manrope typeface for maximum clarity in data-dense environments. The color palette follows an "Enterprise-Grade" scheme: Deep Slate for depth, Forest Green for health, and Enterprise Gold for critical ROI-focused highlights.
